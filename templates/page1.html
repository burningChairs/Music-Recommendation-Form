Shoegaze

Shoegaze blends dreamy guitar textures with hazy vocals and emotional atmosphere. These bands defined the genre for me:

Slowdive

Members: Neil Halstead, Rachel Goswell, Christian Savill, Nick Chaplin, Simon Scott
Notable: Their 1993 album Souvlaki became a cornerstone of shoegaze after being underrated at release. They reunited in 2014 and released a self-titled album in 2017 to critical acclaim.

My Bloody Valentine

Members: Kevin Shields, Bilinda Butcher, Debbie Googe, Colm Ó Cíosóig
Notable: Their 1991 album Loveless is considered one of the most influential alternative albums ever. Known for their use of tremolo and dense textures.

Superheaven

Members: Taylor Madison, Jake Clarke, Joe Kane, Zack Robbins
Notable: Combining shoegaze and grunge, their album Ours Is Chrome (2015) is a heavy, emotional ride. Formerly known as Daylight before rebranding.
